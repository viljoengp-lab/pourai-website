How do I make an Espresso Martini and what's the best vodka to use in Dubai?

Bar-Back

Espresso Martini
50ml vodka · 20ml Kahlua · 30ml fresh espresso · 10ml sugar syrup

Shake hard with ice for 15 seconds. Double strain into chilled martini glass. Garnish with 3 coffee beans.

Best vodka for Dubai venues: A premium French wheat-based vodka is the top choice for luxury service — soft, creamy and highly recognisable to guests. For a more affordable build, established Swedish and Eastern European brands work excellently. Grape-based vodkas are popular in nightlife venues for their lifestyle positioning.

Bartender

Tell me about a premium Speyside 18 Year Old Sherry Oak whisky for a VIP guest.

Bar-Back

Premium Speyside 18 Year Old Sherry Oak whiskies are widely considered the benchmark of their respective ranges — matured exclusively in European oak casks seasoned with Oloroso sherry in Jerez, Spain, for a minimum of 18 years. Each annual release is slightly different, making every bottle unique.

Serving: Always neat. Present the bottle to the guest before pouring. Offer still water on the side — a few drops opens remarkable complexity. Never with ice for this category of expression.
